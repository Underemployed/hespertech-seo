  • Rapid execution avoided delays

High Visibility Mechanical Point Indicator

Partner: Southern Railway, TVM Division

A fully mechanical indicator designed for non-interlocked yards to improve loco pilot safety.

Challenges
  • No visual cue in yards like Irumpanam
  • Visibility issues and risk of delay
  • Need low-maintenance, power-free design
Solution
  • Powerless mechanical system
  • Linked with point lever for live direction
  • Quick setup with high visibility
Impact
  • 12 units deployed successfully
  • Reduced verbal coordination
  • No electrical infra required
  • Model public-private partnership
