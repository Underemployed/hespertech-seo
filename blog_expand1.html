Custom 1-Ton Construction Hoist – 68 Feet (4+G Height)

Client: R Sanjaykumar, Managing Director, SK Builders & Developers

Project Background

SK Builders & Developers approached Hesper Tech Pvt Ltd with a unique challenge: to design a material hoist tailored for high-rise building projects — not just another standard unit, but a re-engineered solution addressing versatility and site-specific constraints.

Client Requirements & Design Challenges

  • Wider Bucket Design: Fit 2 large cement bucket trolleys without spillage or imbalance.
  • Personnel Safety: Ensure stable and secure worker transport.
  • Anchor-Free Mechanism: No permanent anchors; allow transport of 6-meter rods.
  • Unique Layout: Functionally and visually distinct from standard hoists.
  • Cost-Effective Build: Balance affordability and reliability.
  • Tight Deadline: Deliver within 5 months.

Hesper Tech’s Solution

  • Reimagined Structure: Stable, anchor-free framework operating at 68 feet height.
  • Reinforced, Oversized Bucket: Accommodates 2 wide trolleys and long rods.
  • Safety-Centric Features: Cable pulleys, guide rails, and fail-safe motors included.
  • Optimized Load Handling: Smooth lift/lower under variable loads.
  • Rapid Execution: Delivered on time with structural testing and integration.

Impact & Outcome

  • Client’s Vision Realized: Met all spatial, functional, and safety needs.
  • Improved Site Productivity: Faster, more flexible material and labour transport.
  • Anchor-Free Versatility: Easier raw material handling and construction flow.
  • Delivered On Time: Completed within 5-month deadline.

Conclusion

This project showcases Hesper Tech’s ability to transform non-standard requirements into practical, innovative solutions. Working closely with R Sanjaykumar and the SK Builders team, we delivered a custom hoist that set a new benchmark in safety, design, and flexibility for construction hoists.
