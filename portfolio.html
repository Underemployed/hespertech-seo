Engineering Innovations in Action

Development of a 1-Ton Capacity Construction Hoist

Timeline: November 2022 – April 2023

HESPER TECH successfully designed, developed, and delivered a 1-Ton Capacity Construction/Builders Hoist with a height of 68 feet (4+G floors). This project demonstrated our expertise in mechanical engineering and fabrication. The hoist has been operational since installation, proving its reliability and efficiency.

Patent Grant for Automated Braking System

Timeline: February 2024

In February 2024, HESPER TECH achieved a significant intellectual milestone with the granting of a patent titled “Automated Braking System and Method”. This patent, involving the founders of HESPER TECH as applicants, highlights the company’s commitment to innovation and its ability to develop groundbreaking technologies that address real-world challenges.

Collaboration with SCTIMST

The collaboration with Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences and Technology, Trivandrum (SCTIMST) and Government Engineering College, Barton Hill (GECBH) enabled the company to contribute to the transformation of innovative patent concepts into fully functional prototypes, furthering advancements in medical technology.

First Work Order (Jan 2024 - Aug 2024): Developed a prototype for an X-Ray Shield Box with XYZ Translation Stage and Specimen Holder.

Second Work Order (Apr 2024 - Jan 2025): Designed and fabricated a Patient Trolley with a Suspension System.

Fabrication and Erection of Scooping Kiosk for Amul

Timeline: September 2024 - November 2024

HESPER TECH expanded its portfolio by completing vendor registration and securing a work order from Gujarat Co-operative Milk Marketing Federation Limited to fabricate and erect a Scooping Kiosk for Amul at Smart Bazaar, Pazhvangadi, Trivandrum. The project was successfully completed in the final week of November 2024, showcasing the company’s versatility in handling diverse fabrication projects.

Partnership with Thompsons Electromatic Tyre Machines

Timeline: November 2024 - Ongoing

HESPER TECH entered into an official agreement with Thompsons Electromatic Tyre Machines, a Thrissur-based firm specializing in patented tyre retreading machines. Under this collaboration, HESPER TECH provides CAD services, including the generation of CAD models, technical drawings, and renders, using reverse engineering techniques. This partnership will enable Thompsons to enhance its market presence and product offerings, further demonstrating HESPER TECH’s ability to deliver value-added solutions.

Design and Fabrication of a Multifunctional Wheelchair-Cum-Stretcher

Status: Ongoing

HESPER TECH is currently engaged in the design and fabrication of a Multifunctional Wheelchair-Cum-Stretcher with Enhanced Accessibility and Optional Seating Expansion. This innovative project aims to address the needs of individuals with mobility challenges by combining the functionalities of a wheelchair and a stretcher into a single, versatile device. The design focuses on enhanced accessibility, user comfort, and adaptability, with an optional seating expansion feature to accommodate varying user requirements.
